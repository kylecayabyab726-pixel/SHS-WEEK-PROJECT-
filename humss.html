Humanities and Social Sciences

The HUMSS Strand

What is HUMSS?

HUMSS, which stands for Humanities and Social Sciences, is one of LCBA's Senior High School (SHS) programs offered under the Academic Track.

Choosing the HUMSS strand helps students develop strong communication skills, critical and creative thinking, a deeper understanding of society and culture, leadership abilities, and preparation for careers focused on people and communities.

Public speaking and media

Literature and creative expression

History and social issues

Education

Teaching, law, and journalism

What You Learn

HUMSS focuses on understanding people and communities through subjects like:

  • Creative Writing
  • Philippine Politics and Governance
  • World Religions and Belief Systems
  • Sociology and Anthropology
  • Communication and Media Studies
  • Community Engagement and Social Work

Why choose LCBA
